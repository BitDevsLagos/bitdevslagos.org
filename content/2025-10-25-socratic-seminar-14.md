# Topics

### Bitcoin Community Innovation: Project Updates 

MC: [Rita](https://github.com/camillarhi) & [Jodom](https://github.com/okjodom)

The October, 2025 session featured presentations on three innovative projects aimed at improving Bitcoin infrastructure, transparency, and accessibility: **Nodegaze**, **Bitsacco**, and **Minmo**.

---

### 1. [Nodegaze](https://www.nodegaze.com): Lightning Node Observability (Presented by Rita)

Rita introduced Nodegaze, a tool designed for **Lightning Node Observability**.

* **Purpose:** Nodegaze helps node operators **monitor the health and activity** of their Lightning Network node.
* **Demonstration:** Rita ran a live demonstration, connecting her test node (on Polar) to Nodegaze and setting up real-time **Discord notifications**. She then triggered test events, which were instantly delivered to the configured Discord server, showcasing its quick alerting capability. 

---

### 2. [Bitsacco](https://bitsacco.com/): Transparent Community Finance (Presented by Jodom)

Jodom presented Bitsacco, a platform focused on community banking and collaboration, drawing inspiration from the **Sacco model popular in Kenya**.

* **Core Goal:** To leverage Bitcoin technology to enhance the **transparency and accountability** of cooperative finance administrators and operations.

---

### 3. [Minmo](https://dev.minmo.to/): Decentralized P2P Aggregator (Presented by Jodom)

Jodom also introduced Minmo, a platform designed to facilitate peer-to-peer Bitcoin exchange:

* **Function:** Minmo acts as a **decentralized aggregator**, connecting individuals who offer **on-ramping and off-ramping** services for Bitcoin using the **Lightning Network**.

---

The session concluded with a Q&A segment that provided clarity on how these platforms contribute to improving the overall Bitcoin infrastructure and ecosystem.
